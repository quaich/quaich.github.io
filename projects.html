PYxis

PYxis is a python-Tkinter based 2D celestial gravity simulator that I have wrote/am writing for my A-Level computing coursework.
The main goal of the program was to do 3 things:
1. Accurately simulate celestial physics to a simplified extent.
2. Do it efficiently.
3. Do it in style.
